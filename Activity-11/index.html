Ophidian Mango Bliss

Ophidian Mango Bliss by Paris Corner is a fragrance for women and men. Ophidian Mango Bliss was launched in 2024. Top notes are Mango, Lemon, Ginger and Red Berries; middle notes are Jasmine, Coumarin and Woody Notes; base notes are Musk, Agarwood, Oud, Amber and Cypriol Oil or Nagarmotha.


The Ophidian Mango Bliss by Paris Corner is a unisex fragrance that combines fruity and woody elements. It features a zesty blend of mango, ginger, lemon, and red berries, complemented by warm woody notes, coumarin, and jasmine. The base notes of musk, amber, agarwood (oud), and cypriol oil create a lingering aroma.

Ophidian Black Cherry

Ophidian Black Cherry by Paris Corner is a Woody fragrance for women and men. This is a new fragrance. Ophidian Black Cherry was launched in 2024. Top notes are Raspberry and Bergamot; middle notes are Praline, Damask Rose, Heliotrope and Jasmine; base notes are Guaiac Wood, Palo Santo, Ambrettolide, Tonka Bean, Patchouli, Vetiver and Pear.
