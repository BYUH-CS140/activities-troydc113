Atlas

by Lattafa Perfumes

Atlas Lattafa Perfumes

for women and men


Atlas by Lattafa Perfumes is a fragrance for women and men. This is a new fragrance. Atlas was launched in 2024. Top notes are Sea Notes, Salt and Lemon; middle notes are Davana and Iris; base notes are Ambergris, Oakmoss and Sandalwood.


Personal thoughts:

Lattafa Atlas tries to make a statement, but unfortunately, the message gets lost somewhere between a spice rack and a bucket of seawater. It opens with a sharp citrus-and-spice combo that feels like it’s yelling instead of speaking. Almost immediately, it dives into a woody-salty mix that aims for “luxury yacht” but ends up closer to “air freshener at a beach bar bathroom.”

The cedarwood is loud and dry, the salt note leans artificial, and the musk just sits there awkwardly, like someone who wasn’t invited to the party but showed up anyway. It’s trying to be fresh, spicy, and masculine all at once—but instead of a smooth blend, it feels like three colognes arguing over which one gets to dominate.

Performance is strong, but not in a good way—it hangs around like that one friend who doesn't know when to leave. A little goes a long way, and too much goes all the wrong ways.

Unless you really love bold, synthetic wood and a briny edge that doesn’t quite hit the mark, Atlas might be a pass. It's the cologne equivalent of wearing sunglasses indoors: trying way too hard to be cool.
