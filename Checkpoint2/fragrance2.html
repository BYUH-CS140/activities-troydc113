Angel's Share

by Killian

Angels' Share By Kilian

for women and men


Angels' Share by By Kilian is a Oriental Vanilla fragrance for women and men. Angels' Share was launched in 2020. The nose behind this fragrance is Benoist Lapouza. Top note is Cognac; middle notes are Cinnamon, Tonka Bean, Oak and Hedione; base notes are Vanilla, Praline, Sandalwood and Candied Almond.


Personal thoughts:

This thing is amazing on the paper, still too sweet and boozy for my tastes personally. The scent profile is nice, it is just too sweet and boozy for me. The dry down becomes more boozy and less sweet on my skin until there is nothing left but booze on fire. It is still a great smell and I do enjoy it a lot, not more than Black Phantom, but it is still nice.
