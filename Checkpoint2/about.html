About

Welcome to FragranceFan!

My name is Troy De la Cruz, and I made this website in hopes of helping people get into the perfume and fragrance community. I believe that all fragrance has a story to tell. 1 Million Royal Rabanne was my very first fragrance. I wanted to impress my girlfriend at the time so I asked her to come with me to Sephora. We didn't know much about fragrances at the time, so it was basically blind leading the blind.


But your journey doesn't have to be the same as mine. This website offers a curated collection of fragrances, complete with detailed information, my personal reviews, and links to forums for additional insights. Please note that I only feature fragrances I've personally experienced. To suggest a fragrance for me to try, feel free to send a message below!
