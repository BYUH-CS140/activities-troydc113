Psychedelic Love by Initio Parfums Privés is a Floral fragrance for women and men. Psychedelic Love was launched in 2017. Top notes are Ylang-Ylang and Bergamot; middle notes are Heliotrope, Hedione and Bulgarian Rose; base notes are Myrrh and Sandalwood.
