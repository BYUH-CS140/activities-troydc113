Tobacco Collection Rich Warm Addictive

by Zara

Tobacco Collection Rich Warm Addictive 2021 Zara

for men


Tobacco Collection Rich Warm Addictive 2021 by Zara is an Oriental Vanilla fragrance for men. Tobacco Collection Rich Warm Addictive 2021 was launched in 2021. Top note is Rum; middle note is Peony; base note is Bourbon Vanilla.


Personal thoughts:

My bottle purchased late 2024 in Australia smells VERY close to Armani Stronger With You Absolutely, but not a clone. It is even richer, darker, and boozier by a little bit. But it does have a very-slight feminine lean which I can't put my finger on --like an upper-class middle-aged woman feminine lean-- while being very dark and boozy. Nonetheless, I think this is unisex and definitely suits guys too. Either dress dark and ominously or like a CEO while wearing this, otherwise this fragrance will wear you. This is a really good fragrance if you like very rich and boozy scents. Man, this is good.

Smells like a vanilla dessert covered in tobacco smoke
