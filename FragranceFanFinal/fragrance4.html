Ophidian Mango Bliss

by Paris Corner

Ophidian Mango Bliss by Paris Corner

for women and men


Ophidian Mango Bliss by Paris Corner is a fragrance for women and men. Ophidian Mango Bliss was launched in 2024. Top notes are Mango, Lemon, Ginger and Red Berries; middle notes are Jasmine, Coumarin and Woody Notes; base notes are Musk, Agarwood, Oud, Amber and Cypriol Oil or Nagarmotha.


Personal thoughts:

This scent is seriously lovely. Fresh, bright, and absolutely perfect for spring and summer days. It’s totally unisex, not too masculine, not too feminine, just that perfect in-between that’s super easy to wear.

It opens with a light, tropical vibe that kinda reminds me of a mojito: zesty, a little sweet, almost good enough to drink. It’s soft but noticeable, there’s not too much mango in the opening, but as it dries down, this fragrance truly becomes “mango bliss”.
